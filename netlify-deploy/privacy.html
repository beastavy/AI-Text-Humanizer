Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how AI Text Humanizer Pro ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Input Text: We process the text you submit to our Service for the purpose of "humanizing" or transforming it. This processing happens either locally on your device or via our secure cloud servers, depending on your selected mode (Lite vs. Pro). We do not store your input text or the transformed output permanently on our servers.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics, such as the number of words transformed, to help us improve the Service.

Device Information: We may collect information about your device type and operating system to ensure compatibility and optimize performance.

2. Third-Party Services

Our Service integrates with third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

2.1 AdMob (Google)

We use AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use device identifiers and other data to provide personalized ads. For more information, please visit Google's Privacy Policy.

2.2 Cloud Processing

In "Pro Mode", your text is sent to our secure backend servers for advanced processing. This data is transmitted securely and is processed transiently. It is not retained after the transformation is complete.

3. Data Storage

Local History: Your transformation history is stored locally on your device using your device's storage. We do not have access to this data.

4. Security

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. But remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support@obsidianlabs.ai.
