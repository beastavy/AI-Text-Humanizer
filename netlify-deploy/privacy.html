Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 21, 2026

Welcome to AI Text Humanizer Pro. Your privacy is paramount to us. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and web services.

1. Information We Process

Our app offers three modes of text humanization:

  • Lite Mode & Balanced Mode (Local): All text processing is conducted locally on your mobile device. Your text inputs, files, and outputs in these modes never leave your device and are not uploaded to our servers.
  • Pro Mode (Cloud AI): When you use Pro Mode, your text inputs are securely transmitted to our backend server (https://ai-text-humanizer-u7rg.onrender.com) to run advanced NLP algorithms. This text transmission is transient; it is processed in-memory to generate the output and is not permanently saved, stored, or distributed.

2. Data Collection & Analytics

We believe in strict data minimization. We do not require account creation to use the app, and we do not collect personal identifiers (such as names, phone numbers, or emails).

Third-Party Services: The application may include advertising networks (specifically Google AdMob) which use anonymous, temporary advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID) to serve personalized or non-personalized advertisements. These identifiers are subject to the privacy policies of the respective third-party providers.

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate physical, technical, and administrative measures to protect any data processed by the application from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

4. Data Deletion

Since we do not store your text inputs or user accounts on our servers, there is no permanent user data stored by us. Any locally stored text history remains on your device and can be cleared at any time by clearing the application's local storage/cache or uninstalling the app.

5. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for a general audience and are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

7. Contact Us
