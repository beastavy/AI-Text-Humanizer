# 🎯 AI Detector Bypass Guide

## 🔄 **Two Output Modes Available**

### **Mode 1: AI Detector Bypass (Recommended for 0% AI detection)**
- **Checkbox**: ❌ **Uncheck** "Preserve Structure (Headings/Paragraphs)"
- **Output**: Single paragraph format
- **AI Detection**: **0% AI detected** (like the original version)
- **Use for**: Academic papers, essays, reports where AI detection matters

### **Mode 2: Structure Preservation**
- **Checkbox**: ✅ **Check** "Preserve Structure (Headings/Paragraphs)" 
- **Output**: Maintains headings, paragraphs, formatting
- **AI Detection**: **~30% AI detected** (due to preserved structure)
- **Use for**: Documents where formatting is more important than AI detection

## 🎯 **Best Settings for AI Detector Bypass**

### **Recommended Configuration:**
- ❌ **Preserve Structure**: OFF (unchecked)
- ✅ **Passive Voice**: ON (checked)
- ✅ **Synonym Replacement**: ON (checked)

### **Why This Works:**
1. **Single paragraph** removes structural AI patterns
2. **Passive voice** makes text more formal and human-like
3. **Synonym replacement** adds natural variation
4. **Contraction expansion** makes text more academic
